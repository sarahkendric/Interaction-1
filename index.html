This is a piece of 3D art by designer Marc Tudisco. I love his work because it is glossy, otherworldly, and absurd. This piece is also an example of experimenting with textures. The artist uses super shiny material for the hand, and a duller metal for the planet.

This is a poster by Portuguese graphic designer Marisa Passos. The focus of the poster is the distorted typography, and I also really enjoy the cheeky message.

This is the artwork for the single ‘Girls Night Out’ by Charli XCX. The image is blends a real-life set with glossy, abstract 3D art. The effect is futuristic without being cold. I’m also attracted to the monochromatic palette.
